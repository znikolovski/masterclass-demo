Useful information

Quick planning notes for this adventure — elevation, timing, and local context.

Elevation
  • High passes often 3,500–4,500 m
  • Plan acclimatisation before summit days
Best time to visit
  • October–March for stable trekking weather
  • November and March balance daylight and crowds
Weather
  • Expect four seasons in a day
  • Waterproof shell essential; wind 60–100 km/h is common
Culture
  • Spanish primary; English in refugios
  • Respect private estancia boundaries
Permits
  • Book CONAF entry and campsites online
  • Carry passport copies for ranger checks
